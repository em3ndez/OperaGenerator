# Opera Generator Showcase

This directory contains selected examples of complete operas generated by the AI Opera Generator system.

## Featured Operas

### 🎭 Iron Aria: The Lost City of Hartford
**Generated**: September 17, 2025
**Scenes**: 6
**AI Models**: GPT-5 and Claude Opus 4.1 (alternating)
**Illustrations**: GPT-Image-1

A post-apocalyptic opera set in the ruins of Hartford, Connecticut, where nature has reclaimed the city. Follow archaeologist Clara and her team as they discover ancient mechanical wonders and unlock the city's mysterious past through song and spectacle.

**Contents**:
- Complete libretto with all 6 scenes
- 6 AI-generated scene illustrations

---

## About the Opera Generator

This project demonstrates advanced AI collaboration in creative arts:
- **Collaborative Writing**: Two AI models alternate writing scenes, creating unique narrative dynamics
- **Visual Storytelling**: Each scene is illustrated with AI-generated artwork
- **Automatic Formatting**: Libretti are automatically formatted with proper operatic conventions
- **Voice Synthesis**: Optional integration with ElevenLabs for dramatic narration

## Technical Details

Built with:
- Java 21 (Virtual Threads, Records, Pattern Matching)
- LangChain4j 1.5.0
- OpenAI GPT-5 & DALL-E
- Anthropic Claude Opus 4.1
- Google Gemini (for critique generation)
- ElevenLabs (for voice synthesis)
