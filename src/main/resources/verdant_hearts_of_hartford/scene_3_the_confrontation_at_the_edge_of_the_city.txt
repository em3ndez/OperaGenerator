Enter Agent MALCOLM, the baritone, clad in battered government armor, and at his side towers the GIANT SINGING ROBOT, eyes glowing like lanterns.]

**ARABELLA** (voice, Defiant):

**GIANT ROBOT** (bass, Aria, in flawless Verdi Italian):

[The earth trembles with his song. Arabella and Tikano cling together but stand firm.]

**TIKANO** (voice, Resolutely, to Arabella):
> For the city and for love,<br>
> No steel nor state can cow my soul.

**ARABELLA** (voice, Brave, voice rising):
> Then let us match their opera<br>
> With passion wilder than machines!

**MALCOLM** (voice, In disbelief):
> What magic is this?<br>
> The city calls—and nature answers!

**GIANT ROBOT** (bass, Malfunctioning, echoing arias):

Let love be our compass,

Past old Hartford’s ruins,
